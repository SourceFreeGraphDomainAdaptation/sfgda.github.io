GALA: Graph Diffusion-Based Alignment With Jigsaw for Source-Free Domain Adaptation

Data-centric Source-Free Graph Domain Adaptation

1Peking University 2University of California, Los Angeles
TPAMI 2024
Paper arXiv PDF Code BibTeX

TLDR

GALA is a novel source-free graph domain adaptation method that uses graph diffusion models to reconstruct source-style graphs from target data, combined with a graph jigsaw strategy to enhance model robustness. The method effectively addresses domain shift and label scarcity challenges in graph neural networks without requiring access to source data during adaptation. GALA 是一种创新的无源图域适应方法，它使用图扩散模型从目标数据重建源风格图，并结合图拼图策略来增强模型鲁棒性。该方法在不需要在适应过程中访问源数据的情况下，有效解决了图神经网络中的域偏移和标签稀缺挑战。

Rank and Align: Towards Effective Source-free Graph Domain Adaptation

Ranking-based Source-Free Graph Domain Adaptation

Junyu Luo1, Zhiping Xiao2, Yifan Wang3, Xiao Luo2, Jingyang Yuan1, Wei Ju1, Langechuan Liu4, Ming Zhang1
1Peking University 2UCLA 3UIBE 4Anker Innovations
IJCAI 2024
Paper arXiv PDF BibTeX

TLDR

RNA is a novel source-free graph domain adaptation method that combines spectral seriation for ranking graph similarities and harmonic graph alignment for effective knowledge transfer. The method addresses the challenges of label scarcity and domain shifts by leveraging robust pairwise rankings and extracting domain-invariant subgraphs, without requiring access to source domain data. RNA 是一种新颖的无源图域适应方法，它结合了用于图相似度排序的谱序列化和谐波图对齐技术，实现有效的知识迁移。该方法通过利用稳健的成对排序和提取域不变子图来解决标签稀缺和域偏移的挑战，无需访问源域数据。
